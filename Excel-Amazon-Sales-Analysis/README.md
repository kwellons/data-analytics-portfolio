# Amazon Sales Analysis (Excel Project)

## Objective
Analyze e-commerce sales data to identify trends in revenue, order behavior, and top-performing product categories.

## Dataset
Contains Amazon e-commerce sales data including:
- Order ID
- Order date
- Unit price, quantity, discount
- Total sales

## Business Questions
- Which product categories generate the most sales?
- Which states have the highest revenue?
- What are the trends in monthly sales?

## Analysis
- Built pivot tables to summarize sales by product category and time period
- Visualized top-selling categories and trends over time
![Amazon Sales Dashboard](Dashboard.png)

## Key Insights
- Furniture generated most of the profit
- The top 10 states, mainly southern states, contributed to most of the total revenue, indicating strong regional concentration
- Weekly revenue and profit peaked in early January and stabilized throughout February. Proft closely tracked revenue indicating consistent margins throughout the period
